Salsbury Steak

Salsbury Steak is a personal vaforite of mine. Here’s a recipe for you to try it out. Just know that the real thing is orders of magnitude beyond any frozen option!

Ingredients

  • 1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed French onion soup
  • 1 ½ pounds ground beef
  • ½ cup dry bread crumbs
  • 1 egg
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
  • ¼ cup ketchup
  • ¼ cup water
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • ½ teaspoon mustard powder

Directions

  1. Step 1 In a large bowl, mix together 1/3 cup condensed French onion soup with ground beef, bread crumbs, egg, salt and black pepper. Shape into 6 oval patties.

  2. Step 2 In a large skillet over medium-high heat, brown both sides of patties. Pour off excess fat.

  3. Step 3 In a small bowl, blend flour and remaining soup until smooth. Mix in ketchup, water, Worcestershire sauce and mustard powder. Pour over meat in skillet. Cover, and cook for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.

I hope you enjoy this recipe as much as I do! I also recommend a nice nap after.
